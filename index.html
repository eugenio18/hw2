Bio

Eugenio was born in Brazil and raised in South Florida. In 2020, he moved to Chicago, having lived in Boston for the previous 7 years. In the early 2000s, following his family path, Eugenio attended Law School for four years, before taking a hiatus to explore his options. His passion for food, brought him to the food industry, where he spent the last decade as a General Manager leading teams of 40+ employees. The pandemic brought a new light into the industry and Eugenio have decided to pursuit a different passion - technology. He is currently an IT student at Bunker Hill Community College in Boston, and Coding Student at Northwestern University in Chicago.
